Skin Types

Dry Skin

Dry skin lacks moisture and can feel tight, rough, or flaky. It may also be prone to irritation and redness.

Oily Skin

Oily skin is characterized by excess sebum production, leading to a shiny appearance and enlarged pores.

Combination Skin

Combination skin has both oily and dry areas, typically with an oily T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) and dry cheeks.
